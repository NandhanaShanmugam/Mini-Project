National Anthem

Taj Mahal, Agra

Taj mahal is a masterpiece of architectural style in conception, treatment and execution and has unique aesthetic qualities in balance, symmetry and harmonious blending of various elements.

Konark sun temple, Odisha

On the shores of the Bay of Bengal, bathed in the rays of the rising sun, the temple at Konarak is a monumental representation of the sun god Surya's chariot; its 24 wheels are decorated with symbolic designs and it is led by a team of six horses.

Thanjavur big temple, Tamil Nadu

Located in the ancient town of Thanjavur (also, called Tanjore), the Brihadeeswarar Temple is indeed a sight to behold. It is a destination of mysteries and spellbinding architecture.

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Lying in the south of Uttar Pradesh, on the banks of the holy River Ganga, breathes Varanasi, which is believed to be the the oldest living city in the world.

Red Fort, Delhi

Red fort is one of the historic monuments located in Delhi. It is not just about the outer beauty of the fortified wall. You can find many interesting attractions inside the fort like palaces, halls, museums and others.

Hawa Mahal, Rajasthan

Place of winds. Made of red and pink sandstone, Jaipur's signature palace of unusual architecture is a stunning example of local artistry. The top of the Hawa Mahal offers an excellent view over the city.

Golden Temple, Punjab

The Golden temple is located in the holy city of the Sikhs, Amritsar. The Golden temple is famous for its full golden dome, it is one of the most sacred pilgrim spots for Sikhs

Elephanta Caves, Mumbai

Welcome to Elephanta Caves, tucked away in a desolate island locally called Gharapuri (city of caves) 10 kilometres east of the Gateway of India. Through the 19th and 20th Centuries, historians and scholars have been in two minds about their exact period of origin.

Jallianwala Bagh, Punjab

With a sordid history attached to it, the Jallianwala Bagh is also one of the top 10 historical places in India. Located in close proximity to the Golden Temple in Amritsar, Punjab, Jallianwala Bagh is a public garden, which is home to a memorial that commemorates the massacre of people celebrating Baisakhi by the British

Sanchi Stupa, Madhya Pradesh

Amongst the top historical places in India, the Sanchi Stupa dates back to the 3rd century, and is said to be one of the oldest monuments in the country. Constructed in the Buddhist style of architecture, the Sanchi Stupa is also an important Buddhist pilgrimage site and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Mysore Palace, Karnataka

Boasting of the grand Indo-Saracenic style of architecture, the Mysore Palace, located in the city of Mysore in Karnataka, remains one of the most popular historical sites in the country. Complete with opulent domes, gardens, arches, turrets as well as colonnades, the palace was the former residence of the royal family of Mysore.

Mahabalipuram, Tamil Nadu

Mahabalipuram is a small town situated in the southeastern part of Tamil Nadu, stretching between the Bay of Bengal and the Great Salt Lake. It is a renowned representative city of our rich Indian culture and heritage. It is mainly a seaport, which lies 58 km away from the capital city Chennai.
